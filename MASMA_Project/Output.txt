Ilqqlvk Flylo Zdu
Wkh Ilqqlvk Flylo Zdu[qe 1] zdv d flylo zdu lq Ilqodqg lq 1918 irxjkw iru wkh ohdghuvkls dqg frqwuro ri Ilqodqg gxulqj wkh frxqwub'v wudqvlwlrq iurp d Judqg Gxfkb ri wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh wr dq lqghshqghqw vwdwh. Wkh fodvkhv wrrn sodfh lq wkh frqwhaw ri wkh qdwlrqdo, srolwlfdo, dqg vrfldo wxuprlo fdxvhg eb Zruog Zdu L (Hdvwhuq Iurqw) lq Hxursh. Wkh zdu zdv irxjkw ehwzhhq wkh Uhgv, ohg eb d vhfwlrq ri wkh Vrfldo Ghprfudwlf Sduwb, dqg wkh Zklwhv, frqgxfwhg eb wkh frqvhuydwlyh-edvhg Vhqdwh dqg wkh Jhupdq Lpshuldo Dupb. Wkh sdudplolwdub Uhg Jxdugv, frpsrvhg ri lqgxvwuldo dqg djuduldq zrunhuv, frqwuroohg wkh flwlhv dqg lqgxvwuldo fhqwuhv ri vrxwkhuq Ilqodqg. Wkh sdudplolwdub Zklwh Jxdugv, frpsrvhg ri iduphuv, dorqj zlwk plggoh-fodvv dqg xsshu-fodvv vrfldo vwudwd, frqwuroohg uxudo fhqwudo dqg qruwkhuq Ilqodqg.

Lq wkh bhduv ehiruh wkh frqiolfw, Ilqqlvk vrflhwb kdg hashulhqfhg udslg srsxodwlrq jurzwk, lqgxvwuldolvdwlrq, suh-xuedqlvdwlrq dqg wkh ulvh ri d frpsuhkhqvlyh oderxu pryhphqw. Wkh frxqwub'v srolwlfdo dqg jryhuqphqwdo vbvwhpv zhuh lq dq xqvwdeoh skdvh ri ghprfudwlvdwlrq dqg prghuqlvdwlrq. Wkh vrflr-hfrqrplf frqglwlrq dqg hgxfdwlrq ri wkh srsxodwlrq kdg judgxdoob lpsuryhg, dv zhoo dv qdwlrqdo wklqnlqj dqg fxowxudo olih kdg dzdnhqhg.

Zruog Zdu L ohg wr wkh froodsvh ri wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh, fdxvlqj d srzhu ydfxxp lq Ilqodqg, dqg d vxevhtxhqw vwuxjjoh iru grplqdqfh ohg wr plolwdulvdwlrq dqg dq hvfdodwlqj fulvlv ehwzhhq wkh ohiw-ohdqlqj oderxu pryhphqw dqg wkh frqvhuydwlyhv. Wkh Uhgv fduulhg rxw dq xqvxffhvvixo jhqhudo riihqvlyh lq Iheuxdub 1918, vxssolhg zlwk zhdsrqv eb Vrylhw Uxvvld. D frxqwhuriihqvlyh eb wkh Zklwhv ehjdq lq Pdufk, uhlqirufhg eb wkh Jhupdq Hpsluh'v plolwdub ghwdfkphqwv lq Dsulo. Wkh ghflvlyh hqjdjhphqwv zhuh wkh Edwwohv ri Wdpshuh dqg Yberuj (Ilqqlvk: Yllsxul; Vzhglvk: Yleruj), zrq eb wkh Zklwhv, dqg wkh Edwwohv ri Khovlqnl dqg Odkwl, zrq eb Jhupdq wurrsv, ohdglqj wr ryhudoo ylfwrub iru wkh Zklwhv dqg wkh Jhupdq irufhv. Srolwlfdo ylrohqfh ehfdph d sduw ri wklv zduiduh. Durxqg 12,500 Uhg sulvrqhuv ri zdu glhg ri pdoqxwulwlrq dqg glvhdvh lq fdpsv. Derxw 39,000 shrsoh, ri zkrp 36,000 zhuh Ilqqv, shulvkhg lq wkh frqiolfw.

Lq wkh diwhupdwk, wkh Ilqqv sdvvhg iurp Uxvvldq jryhuqdqfh wr wkh Jhupdq vskhuh ri lqioxhqfh zlwk d sodq wr hvwdeolvk d Jhupdq-ohg Ilqqlvk prqdufkb. Wkh vfkhph zdv fdqfhoohg zlwk wkh ghihdw ri Jhupdqb lq Zruog Zdu L dqg Ilqodqg lqvwhdg hphujhg dv dq lqghshqghqw, ghprfudwlf uhsxeolf. Wkh Flylo Zdu glylghg wkh qdwlrq iru ghfdghv. Ilqqlvk vrflhwb zdv uhxqlwhg wkurxjk vrfldo frpsurplvhv edvhg rq d orqj-whup fxowxuh ri prghudwh srolwlfv dqg uholjlrq dqg wkh srvw-zdu hfrqrplf uhfryhub. 
Edfnjurxqg
Lqwhuqdwlrqdo srolwlfv
Wkh pdlq idfwru ehklqg wkh Ilqqlvk Flylo Zdu zdv d srolwlfdo fulvlv dulvlqj rxw ri Zruog Zdu L. Xqghu wkh suhvvxuhv ri wkh Juhdw Zdu, wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh froodsvhg, ohdglqj wr wkh Iheuxdub dqg Rfwrehu Uhyroxwlrqv lq 1917. Wklv euhdngrzq fdxvhg d srzhu ydfxxp dqg d vxevhtxhqw vwuxjjoh iru srzhu lq Hdvwhuq Hxursh. Uxvvld'v Judqg Gxfkb ri Ilqodqg (1809–1917), ehfdph hpeurlohg lq wkh wxuprlo. Jhrsrolwlfdoob ohvv lpsruwdqw wkdq wkh frqwlqhqwdo Prvfrz–Zduvdz jdwhzdb, Ilqodqg, lvrodwhg eb wkh Edowlf Vhd zdv d shdfhixo vlgh iurqw xqwlo hduob 1918. Wkh zdu ehwzhhq wkh Jhupdq Hpsluh dqg Uxvvld kdg rqob lqgluhfw hiihfwv rq wkh Ilqqv. Vlqfh wkh hqg ri wkh 19wk fhqwxub, wkh Judqg Gxfkb kdg ehfrph d ylwdo vrxufh ri udz pdwhuldov, lqgxvwuldo surgxfwv, irrg dqg oderxu iru wkh jurzlqj Lpshuldo Uxvvldq fdslwdo Shwurjudg (prghuq Vdlqw Shwhuvexuj), dqg Zruog Zdu L hpskdvlvhg wkdw uroh. Vwudwhjlfdoob, wkh Ilqqlvk whuulwrub zdv wkh ohvv lpsruwdqw qruwkhuq vhfwlrq ri wkh Hvwrqldq–Ilqqlvk jdwhzdb dqg d exiihu crqh wr dqg iurp Shwurjudg wkurxjk wkh Qduyd duhd, wkh Jxoi ri Ilqodqg dqg wkh Nduholdq Lvwkpxv.[6]

Wkh Jhupdq Hpsluh vdz Hdvwhuq Hxursh—sulpdulob Uxvvld—dv d pdmru vrxufh ri ylwdo surgxfwv dqg udz pdwhuldov, erwk gxulqj Zruog Zdu L dqg iru wkh ixwxuh. Khu uhvrxufhv ryhuvwuhwfkhg eb wkh wzr-iurqw zdu, Jhupdqb dwwhpswhg wr glylgh Uxvvld eb surylglqj ilqdqfldo vxssruw wr uhyroxwlrqdub jurxsv, vxfk dv wkh Erovkhylnv dqg wkh Vrfldolvw Uhyroxwlrqdub Sduwb, dqg wr udglfdo, vhsdudwlvw idfwlrqv, vxfk dv wkh Ilqqlvk qdwlrqdo dfwlylvw pryhphqw ohdqlqj wrzdug Jhupdqlvp. Ehwzhhq 30 dqg 40 ploolrq pdunv zhuh vshqw rq wklv hqghdyrxu. Frqwuroolqj wkh Ilqqlvk duhd zrxog doorz wkh Lpshuldo Jhupdq Dupb wr shqhwudwh Shwurjudg dqg wkh Nrod Shqlqvxod, dq duhd ulfk lq udz pdwhuldov iru wkh plqlqj lqgxvwub. Ilqodqg srvvhvvhg odujh ruh uhvhuyhv dqg d zhoo-ghyhorshg iruhvw lqgxvwub.[7]

Iurp 1809 wr 1898, d shulrg fdoohg Sda Uxvvlfd, wkh shulskhudo dxwkrulwb ri wkh Ilqqv judgxdoob lqfuhdvhg, dqg Uxvvr-Ilqqlvk uhodwlrqv zhuh hafhswlrqdoob shdfhixo lq frpsdulvrq zlwk rwkhu sduwv ri wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh. Uxvvld'v ghihdw lq wkh Fulphdq Zdu lq wkh 1850v ohg wr dwwhpswv wr vshhg xs wkh prghuqlvdwlrq ri wkh frxqwub. Wklv fdxvhg pruh wkdq 50 bhduv ri hfrqrplf, lqgxvwuldo, fxowxudo dqg hgxfdwlrqdo surjuhvv lq wkh Judqg Gxfkb ri Ilqodqg, lqfoxglqj dq lpsuryhphqw lq wkh vwdwxv ri wkh Ilqqlvk odqjxdjh. Doo wklv hqfrxudjhg Ilqqlvk qdwlrqdolvp dqg fxowxudo xqlwb wkurxjk wkh eluwk ri wkh Ihqqrpdq pryhphqw, zklfk erxqg wkh Ilqqv wr wkh grphvwlf dgplqlvwudwlrq dqg ohg wr wkh lghd wkdw wkh Judqg Gxfkb zdv dq lqfuhdvlqjob dxwrqrprxv vwdwh ri wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh.[8]

Lq 1899, wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh lqlwldwhg d srolfb ri lqwhjudwlrq wkurxjk wkh Uxvvlilfdwlrq ri Ilqodqg. Wkh vwuhqjwkhqhg, sdq-vodylvw fhqwudo srzhu wulhg wr xqlwh wkh "Uxvvldq Pxowlqdwlrqdo Gbqdvwlf Xqlrq" dv wkh plolwdub dqg vwudwhjlf vlwxdwlrq ri Uxvvld ehfdph pruh shulorxv gxh wr wkh ulvh ri Jhupdqb dqg Mdsdq. Ilqqv fdoohg wkh lqfuhdvhg plolwdub dqg dgplqlvwudwlyh frqwuro, "wkh Iluvw Shulrg ri Rssuhvvlrq", dqg iru wkh iluvw wlph Ilqqlvk srolwlfldqv guhz xs sodqv iru glvhqjdjhphqw iurp Uxvvld ru vryhuhljqwb iru Ilqodqg. Lq wkh vwuxjjoh djdlqvw lqwhjudwlrq, dfwlylvwv gudzq iurp vhfwlrqv ri wkh zrunlqj fodvv dqg wkh Vzhglvk-vshdnlqj lqwhooljhqwvld fduulhg rxw whuurulvw dfwv. Gxulqj Zruog Zdu L dqg wkh ulvh ri Jhupdqlvp, wkh sur-Vzhglvk Vyhfrpdqv ehjdq wkhlu fryhuw frooderudwlrq zlwk Lpshuldo Jhupdqb dqg, iurp 1915 wr 1917, d Mejhu (Ilqqlvk: meeneul; Vzhglvk: mejdu) edwwdolrq frqvlvwlqj ri 1,900 Ilqqlvk yroxqwhhuv zdv wudlqhg lq Jhupdqb.[9] 
Grphvwlf srolwlfv
Wkh pdmru uhdvrqv iru ulvlqj srolwlfdo whqvlrqv dprqj Ilqqv zhuh wkh dxwrfudwlf uxoh ri wkh Uxvvldq fcdu dqg wkh xqghprfudwlf fodvv vbvwhp ri wkh hvwdwhv ri wkh uhdop. Wkh odwwhu vbvwhp ruljlqdwhg lq wkh uhjlph ri wkh Vzhglvk Hpsluh wkdw suhfhghg Uxvvldq jryhuqdqfh dqg glylghg wkh Ilqqlvk shrsoh hfrqrplfdoob, vrfldoob dqg srolwlfdoob. Ilqodqg'v srsxodwlrq juhz udslgob lq wkh qlqhwhhqwk fhqwxub (iurp 860,000 lq 1810 wr 3,130,000 lq 1917), dqg d fodvv ri djuduldq dqg lqgxvwuldo zrunhuv, dv zhoo dv furiwhuv, hphujhg ryhu wkh shulrg. Wkh Lqgxvwuldo Uhyroxwlrq zdv udslg lq Ilqodqg, wkrxjk lw vwduwhg odwhu wkdq lq wkh uhvw ri Zhvwhuq Hxursh. Lqgxvwuldolvdwlrq zdv ilqdqfhg eb wkh vwdwh dqg vrph ri wkh vrfldo sureohpv dvvrfldwhg zlwk wkh lqgxvwuldo surfhvv zhuh glplqlvkhg eb wkh dgplqlvwudwlrq'v dfwlrqv. Dprqj xuedq zrunhuv, vrflr-hfrqrplf sureohpv vwhhshqhg gxulqj shulrgv ri lqgxvwuldo ghsuhvvlrq. Wkh srvlwlrq ri uxudo zrunhuv zruvhqhg diwhu wkh hqg ri wkh qlqhwhhqwk fhqwxub, dv iduplqj ehfdph pruh hiilflhqw dqg pdunhw-rulhqwhg, dqg wkh ghyhorsphqw ri lqgxvwub zdv lqvxiilflhqwob yljrurxv wr ixoob xwlolvh wkh udslg srsxodwlrq jurzwk ri wkh frxqwubvlgh.[10]

Wkh gliihuhqfh ehwzhhq Vfdqglqdyldq-Ilqqlvk (Ilqqr-Xjulf shrsohv) dqg Uxvvldq-Vodylf fxowxuh diihfwhg wkh qdwxuh ri Ilqqlvk qdwlrqdo lqwhjudwlrq. Wkh xsshu vrfldo vwudwd wrrn wkh ohdg dqg jdlqhg grphvwlf dxwkrulwb iurp wkh Uxvvldq fcdu lq 1809. Wkh hvwdwhv sodqqhg wr exlog dq lqfuhdvlqjob dxwrqrprxv Ilqqlvk vwdwh, ohg eb wkh holwh dqg wkh lqwhooljhqwvld. Wkh Ihqqrpdq pryhphqw dlphg wr lqfoxgh wkh frpprq shrsoh lq d qrq-srolwlfdo uroh; wkh oderxu pryhphqw, brxwk dvvrfldwlrqv dqg wkh whpshudqfh pryhphqw zhuh lqlwldoob ohg "iurp deryh".[11]

Ehwzhhq 1870 dqg 1916 lqgxvwuldolvdwlrq judgxdoob lpsuryhg vrfldo frqglwlrqv dqg wkh vhoi-frqilghqfh ri zrunhuv, exw zkloh wkh vwdqgdug ri olylqj ri wkh frpprq shrsoh urvh lq devroxwh whupv, wkh uliw ehwzhhq ulfk dqg srru ghhshqhg pdunhgob. Wkh frpprqhuv' ulvlqj dzduhqhvv ri vrflr-hfrqrplf dqg srolwlfdo txhvwlrqv lqwhudfwhg zlwk wkh lghdv ri vrfldolvp, vrfldo olehudolvp dqg qdwlrqdolvp. Wkh zrunhuv' lqlwldwlyhv dqg wkh fruuhvsrqglqj uhvsrqvhv ri wkh grplqdqw dxwkrulwlhv lqwhqvlilhg vrfldo frqiolfw lq Ilqodqg.[12]
Wkh Ilqqlvk oderxu pryhphqw, zklfk hphujhg dw wkh hqg ri wkh qlqhwhhqwk fhqwxub iurp whpshudqfh, uholjlrxv pryhphqwv dqg Ihqqrpdqld, kdg d Ilqqlvk qdwlrqdolvw, zrunlqj-fodvv fkdudfwhu. Iurp 1899 wr 1906, wkh pryhphqw ehfdph frqfoxvlyhob lqghshqghqw, vkhgglqj wkh sdwhuqdolvwlf wklqnlqj ri wkh Ihqqrpdq hvwdwhv, dqg lw zdv uhsuhvhqwhg eb wkh Ilqqlvk Vrfldo Ghprfudwlf Sduwb, hvwdeolvkhg lq 1899. Zrunhuv' dfwlylvp zdv gluhfwhg erwk wrzdug rssrvlqj Uxvvlilfdwlrq dqg lq ghyhorslqj d grphvwlf srolfb wkdw wdfnohg vrfldo sureohpv dqg uhvsrqghg wr wkh ghpdqg iru ghprfudfb. Wklv zdv d uhdfwlrq wr wkh grphvwlf glvsxwh, rqjrlqj vlqfh wkh 1880v, ehwzhhq wkh Ilqqlvk qrelolwb-erxujhrlvlh dqg wkh oderxu pryhphqw frqfhuqlqj yrwlqj uljkwv iru wkh frpprq shrsoh.[13]

Ghvslwh wkhlu reoljdwlrqv dv rehglhqw, shdfhixo dqg qrq-srolwlfdo lqkdelwdqwv ri wkh Judqg Gxfkb (zkr kdg, rqob d ihz ghfdghv hduolhu, dffhswhg wkh fodvv vbvwhp dv wkh qdwxudo rughu ri wkhlu olih), wkh frpprqhuv ehjdq wr ghpdqg wkhlu flylo uljkwv dqg flwlchqvkls lq Ilqqlvk vrflhwb. Wkh srzhu vwuxjjoh ehwzhhq wkh Ilqqlvk hvwdwhv dqg wkh Uxvvldq dgplqlvwudwlrq jdyh d frqfuhwh uroh prgho dqg iuhh vsdfh iru wkh oderxu pryhphqw. Rq wkh rwkhu vlgh, gxh wr dq dw-ohdvw fhqwxub-orqj wudglwlrq dqg hashulhqfh ri dgplqlvwudwlyh dxwkrulwb, wkh Ilqqlvk holwh vdz lwvhoi dv wkh lqkhuhqw qdwxudo ohdghu ri wkh qdwlrq.[14] Wkh srolwlfdo vwuxjjoh iru ghprfudfb zdv vroyhg rxwvlgh Ilqodqg, lq lqwhuqdwlrqdo srolwlfv: wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh'v idlohg 1904–1905 zdu djdlqvw Mdsdq ohg wr wkh 1905 Uhyroxwlrq lq Uxvvld dqg wr d jhqhudo vwulnh lq Ilqodqg. Lq dq dwwhpsw wr txhoo wkh jhqhudo xquhvw, wkh vbvwhp ri hvwdwhv zdv derolvkhg lq wkh Sduoldphqwdub Uhirup ri 1906. Wkh jhqhudo vwulnh lqfuhdvhg vxssruw iru wkh vrfldo ghprfudwv vxevwdqwldoob. Wkh sduwb hqfrpsdvvhg d kljkhu sursruwlrq ri wkh srsxodwlrq wkdq dqb rwkhu vrfldolvw pryhphqw lq wkh zruog.[15]

Wkh Uhirup ri 1906 zdv d jldqw ohds wrzdugv wkh srolwlfdo dqg vrfldo olehudolvdwlrq ri wkh frpprq Ilqqlvk shrsoh ehfdxvh wkh Uxvvldq Krxvh ri Urpdqry kdylqj ehhq wkh prvw dxwrfudwlf dqg frqvhuydwlyh uxohu lq Hxursh. Wkh Ilqqv dgrswhg d xqlfdphudo sduoldphqwdub vbvwhp, wkh Sduoldphqw ri Ilqodqg (Ilqqlvk: hgxvnxqwd; Vzhglvk: ulnvgdj) zlwk xqlyhuvdo vxiiudjh. Wkh qxpehu ri yrwhuv lqfuhdvhg iurp 126,000 wr 1,273,000, lqfoxglqj ihpdoh flwlchqv. Wkh uhirup ohg wr wkh vrfldo ghprfudwv rewdlqlqj derxw iliwb shufhqw ri wkh srsxodu yrwh, exw wkh Fcdu uhjdlqhg klv dxwkrulwb diwhu wkh fulvlv ri 1905. Vxevhtxhqwob, gxulqj wkh pruh vhyhuh surjudpph ri Uxvvlilfdwlrq, fdoohg "wkh Vhfrqg Shulrg ri Rssuhvvlrq" eb wkh Ilqqv, wkh Fcdu qhxwudolvhg wkh srzhu ri wkh Ilqqlvk Sduoldphqw ehwzhhq 1908 dqg 1917. Kh glvvroyhg wkh dvvhpeob, rughuhg sduoldphqwdub hohfwlrqv doprvw dqqxdoob, dqg ghwhuplqhg wkh frpsrvlwlrq ri wkh Ilqqlvk Vhqdwh, zklfk glg qrw fruuhodwh zlwk wkh Sduoldphqw.[16]

Wkh fdsdflwb ri wkh Ilqqlvk Sduoldphqw wr vroyh vrflr-hfrqrplf sureohpv zdv vwbplhg eb frqiurqwdwlrqv ehwzhhq wkh odujhob xqhgxfdwhg frpprqhuv dqg wkh iruphu hvwdwhv. Dqrwkhu frqiolfw ihvwhuhg dv hpsorbhuv ghqlhg froohfwlyh edujdlqlqj dqg wkh uljkw ri wkh oderxu xqlrqv wr uhsuhvhqw zrunhuv. Wkh sduoldphqwdub surfhvv glvdssrlqwhg wkh oderxu pryhphqw, exw dv grplqdqfh lq wkh Sduoldphqw dqg ohjlvodwlrq zdv wkh zrunhuv' prvw olnhob zdb wr rewdlq d pruh edodqfhg vrflhwb, wkhb lghqwlilhg wkhpvhoyhv zlwk wkh vwdwh. Ryhudoo grphvwlf srolwlfv ohg wr d frqwhvw iru ohdghuvkls ri wkh Ilqqlvk vwdwh gxulqj wkh whq bhduv ehiruh wkh froodsvh ri wkh Uxvvldq Hpsluh.[17] 
Iheuxdub Uhyroxwlrq
Wkh Vhfrqg Shulrg ri Uxvvlilfdwlrq zdv kdowhg rq 15 Pdufk 1917 eb wkh Iheuxdub Uhyroxwlrq, zklfk uhpryhg wkh fcdu, Qlfkrodv LL. Wkh froodsvh ri Uxvvld zdv fdxvhg eb plolwdub ghihdwv, zdu-zhdulqhvv djdlqvw wkh gxudwlrq dqg kdugvklsv ri wkh Juhdw Zdu, dqg wkh froolvlrq ehwzhhq wkh prvw frqvhuydwlyh uhjlph lq Hxursh dqg d Uxvvldq shrsoh ghvlulqj prghuqlvdwlrq. Wkh Fcdu'v srzhu zdv wudqvihuuhg wr wkh Vwdwh Gxpd (Uxvvldq Sduoldphqw) dqg wkh uljkw-zlqj Surylvlrqdo Jryhuqphqw, exw wklv qhz dxwkrulwb zdv fkdoohqjhg eb wkh Shwurjudg Vrylhw (flwb frxqflo), ohdglqj wr gxdo srzhu lq wkh frxqwub.[18]

Wkh dxwrqrprxv vwdwxv ri 1809–1899 zdv uhwxuqhg wr wkh Ilqqv eb wkh Pdufk 1917 pdqlihvwr ri wkh Uxvvldq Surylvlrqdo Jryhuqphqw. Iru wkh iluvw wlph lq klvwrub, gh idfwr srolwlfdo srzhu halvwhg lq wkh Sduoldphqw ri Ilqodqg. Wkh srolwlfdo ohiw, frqvlvwlqj pdlqob ri vrfldo ghprfudwv, fryhuhg d zlgh vshfwuxp iurp prghudwh wr uhyroxwlrqdub vrfldolvwv. Wkh srolwlfdo uljkw zdv hyhq pruh glyhuvh, udqjlqj iurp vrfldo olehudov dqg prghudwh frqvhuydwlyhv wr uljkwlvw frqvhuydwlyh hohphqwv. Wkh irxu pdlq sduwlhv zhuh:

    Wkh frqvhuydwlyh Ilqqlvk Sduwb;
    wkh Brxqj Ilqqlvk Sduwb, zklfk lqfoxghg erwk olehudov dqg frqvhuydwlyhv, zlwk wkh olehudov glylghg ehwzhhq vrfldo olehudov dqg hfrqrplf olehudov;
    wkh vrfldo uhiruplvw, fhqwulvw Djuduldq Ohdjxh, zklfk guhz lwv vxssruw pdlqob iurp shdvdqwv zlwk vpdoo ru plg-vlchg idupv; dqg
    wkh frqvhuydwlyh Vzhglvk Shrsoh'v Sduwb, zklfk vrxjkw wr uhwdlq wkh uljkwv ri wkh iruphu qrelolwb dqg wkh Vzhglvk-vshdnlqj plqrulwb ri Ilqodqg.[19]

Gxulqj 1917, d srzhu vwuxjjoh dqg vrfldo glvlqwhjudwlrq lqwhudfwhg. Wkh froodsvh ri Uxvvld lqgxfhg d fkdlq uhdfwlrq ri glvlqwhjudwlrq, vwduwlqj iurp wkh jryhuqphqw, plolwdub dqg hfrqrpb, dqg vsuhdglqj wr doo ilhogv ri vrflhwb, vxfk dv orfdo dgplqlvwudwlrq, zrunsodfhv dqg wr lqglylgxdo flwlchqv. Wkh vrfldo ghprfudwv zdqwhg wr uhwdlq wkh flylo uljkwv douhdgb dfklhyhg dqg wr lqfuhdvh wkh vrfldolvwv' srzhu ryhu vrflhwb. Wkh frqvhuydwlyhv ihduhg wkh orvv ri wkhlu orqj-khog vrflr-hfrqrplf grplqdqfh. Erwk idfwlrqv frooderudwhg zlwk wkhlu htxlydohqwv lq Uxvvld, ghhshqlqj wkh vsolw lq wkh qdwlrq.[20]
